About the Institution

Somaiya Vidyavihar University (SVU), established in 2019, is a premier private university located in Mumbai. With a legacy of over 80 years, the Somaiya Trust provides world-class education across engineering, medicine, management, science, and humanities.

  • Campus: 60+ acres of green and modern infrastructure
  • Students: 39,000+ learners across 34 institutions
  • Academics: UG, PG, PhD with flexible curriculum
  • Placements: Highest package up to ₹25 LPA

Why Choose KJ Somaiya?

The institution emphasizes innovation, research, and industry exposure. Students are encouraged to develop technical excellence along with ethical values and leadership qualities.
